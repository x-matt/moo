### Mermaid

- I/O:
  - shape: 平行四边形 - `[/IN_OR_OUT/]`
  - color: 红色 - `fill:#fbbfbc,stroke:#f98e8b`
- subGraph:
  - shape: 圆角四边形 - `rx:8,ry:8`
  - color: 橙色 - `fill:#feebd3,stroke:#ffba6b`
- node:
  - shape: 圆角矩形 - `(rect)`
  - color: 绿色 - `%%{init: {'theme':'forest'}}%%`
